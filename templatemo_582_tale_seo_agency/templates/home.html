Welcome to EcoPeace







EcoPeace is a non-profit organization that works to create peace in the Middle East while taking care of our planet. We bring together Israeli, Palestinian, and Jordanian environmentalists to work together towards a shared future.

As a part of our extended social reach program, EcoPeace has collaborated and connected with other companies with a similar vision to ours. We aim to reach as many people as possible from our target audience and achieve our beautiful vision: a step closer to peace, and an improved climate in our surroundings.

A little about one of our prestigious collaborators: One of our collaborations is with a European Studies program that is launched partly in Dusseldorf, Germany, at the Heinrich Heine University. The program was founded in 2004 by Ambassador Avi Primor, together with HRH Prince El-Hassan bin Talal, who founded the Royal Scientific Society in Amman, and Prof. Sari Nusseibeh, the president of Al-Quds University in East Jerusalem.

The European Studies program is also given beneficial services from us, such as bringing our teams to speak to the students and bringing groups of students willing to experience the Middle East, its climates, and its cultures.
